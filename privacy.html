2. What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Specifically:

  • Log data: GitHub Pages (our hosting provider) automatically collects standard server logs including IP address, browser type, referring page, and pages visited. We do not have direct access to these logs and do not use them to identify individuals.
  • Cookies: This site may use a cookie to store your theme preference. This cookie is essential for site functionality and is not used for tracking.

All tool operations (JSON formatting, encoding, hashing, etc.) run entirely in your browser. Your data is never sent to any server.
